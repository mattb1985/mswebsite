About Us

McIver & Smith Fabricators,inc. is a Houston based company that has been providing excellent work in ASME Code Div.1 Pressure vessel Fabrication. Mciver & Smith Fabricators, Inc. has been fabricating pressure vessels, towers and columns for the oil and petrochemical industry since 1946 with a reputation for excellent quality. With over 70 years of excellent service, McIver & Smith Fabricators is regarded as a preferred supplier in the industry. As one of the oldest Fabricators around McIver & Smith Fabricators continues to be a leader in the pressure vessel fabricating industry.

McIver & Smith holds the following stamps

  • - ASME "S" Stamp
  • - ASME "U" Stamp
  • - ASME "PP" Stamp
  • - ASME "R" Stamp for repair or changes needed on existing equipment

Our History
McIver & Smith Fabricators, Inc, founded in 1946 by John McIver & P.K. Smith, fabricates pressure vessels, towers, and reactors for the oil and petrochemical industry. The Company was acquired by R.L. Boles, Sr. in 1991. Mr. Boles has been an employee of McIver & Smith since the age of 19 and now has over 50 years experience in pressure vessel fabrication. With over 60 years of service, McIver & Smith is regarded as a preferred supplier in the industry with a successful history of quality workmanship. As one of the oldest fabricators around, McIver continues to be a leader in the industry.
